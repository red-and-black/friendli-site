Finding your people can be hard!

You're at a conference, in a sea of people.

How do you know who there shares your interests and passions, and wants to meet new people?

It's hard to tell just by looking at someone.

We've been in that situation countless times ourselves, so we built GoodChat.

Use it to search for and contact other likeminded people at your conference.

At last, an easy way to meet people at conferences!

Also, it's free, and we won't sell your data. We only made this app to make the world a slightly better place.
